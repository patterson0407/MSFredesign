Our behavioral commitments

MSF is committed to ensuring a respectful, safe, and inclusive work environment. We have zero tolerance for sexual exploitation, abuse, and harassment. All staff members are expected to uphold our code of conduct and behavioral commitments in all aspects of our work.

We are dedicated to protecting the people we serve and our staff from any form of abuse, and to creating an environment where everyone can work with dignity and respect.

How to apply

Learn about our application and recruitment process for field and office positions.

General information

MSF recruits professionals from around the world to work in our field operations and offices. Our recruitment process ensures we find skilled, committed individuals who share our humanitarian values.

Applications are reviewed carefully, and candidates are assessed based on their qualifications, experience, and alignment with MSF's mission.

Application process

1

Interview

Submit your application through our online portal. Include your CV, cover letter, and any relevant certifications.

2

Welcome Days

Qualified candidates are invited to an interview to discuss their experience, skills, and motivations for joining MSF.

3

Pool

Successful candidates join our pool of available staff, ready for deployment when suitable positions become available.

MSF provides medical humanitarian assistance in more than 70 countries. We go to the places where others cannot or choose not to go.

Our activities

Medical care

We provide primary healthcare, surgery, maternal care, and treatment for diseases like malaria, tuberculosis, and HIV/AIDS.

Emergency response

When disaster strikes, we deploy medical teams rapidly to provide life-saving care in conflict zones and natural disasters.

Vaccination campaigns

We conduct mass vaccination campaigns to prevent disease outbreaks and protect vulnerable populations.

Nutrition programs

We treat malnutrition and provide nutritional support to children and families facing food insecurity.

Mental health

We provide psychological support and mental healthcare to people affected by trauma, conflict, and displacement.

Surgery

Our surgical teams perform life-saving operations in hospitals and mobile surgical units in crisis zones.
